A few years ago I fell in love with a little program called Nocturne. Unfortunately, the author abandoned it and changes to new versions of Mac OS X made the old version break. Fortunately, the author released the source code under the highly permissive Apache 2.0 license. Today, after a bit of tweaking around, I am proud to present:

Tranquility

 

Peace and quiet for your eyes.

Tranquility lets you work at night or in a dark room. It prevents you from being a nuisance to others around you at night or in a dark room.

Ever tried to sleep on an airplane next to someone working on their computer, brightness at full blast? Don't be that person!

Ever tried to use your laptop while camping, only to find out that you're night blind? Keep your night vision while still coding!

 

Download Tranquility or view the source code at GitHub
